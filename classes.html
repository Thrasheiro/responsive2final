Austin Community College offers the skills you need to get ahead in the workplace at a price you can afford.

Survey of User Experience Design

A survey course focusing on the business of digital product design. Students will research, discuss, and define a range of topics selected from the following: content strategy; brand strategy; interaction design; interface design; UX visual design; UX research; UX development; product management; product and business strategy; product features; specs; metrics; what User Experience is; what UX Designers do; how UXers think; and the tools UX'ers use.

Prototyping 1

A study of the tools and techniques used to create rapid prototypes for websites, web applications, and native apps based on industry standards. May include Keynote, Balsamic, Axure, Omni tools, and open source tools.
